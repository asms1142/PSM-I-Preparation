Scrum is a lightweight framework that helps people, team and organizations generate value through adaptive solutions for complex problems.

In a nutshel, scrum require a Scrum Master to foster an environment where

* A product owner orders the work for a complex problem into a product backlog.
* The scrum team turns a selection of the work into an increment of value during a sprint.
* The scrum team and its stakeholders inspect the result of the sprint and adjust for the next sprint
